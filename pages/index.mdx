# nova neace

Hi, I'm Nova

I was born and raised in Merritt Island, Florida with three brothers and my loving parents. As a child I often had trouble connecting with other kids I knew in real life which led to me taking to the internet to make connections with people who had similar interests. I ended up making a lot of friends online who supported exploring my interests in technology and video game design. As for how I got into software development, I have my mom to thank. She supported my interest in designing video games and when I joined the computer science program at my high school she was always supportive of my frankly very nerdy interests. As previously mentioned, I have always had an interest in the web and technology. I have always spent a lot of time on the internet and thinking about online spaces and communities that I participate in. I found that Information Science was the perfect major to marry my interests together. 

I am studying Information Science with a concentration in Web Development so that I will be equipped with the necessary skills to solve digital problems and create helpful and interesting software and media. This field has provided a challenge, peaked my interest in a fun way, and provided me with the opportunity to make a meaningful living for myself. Aside from developing technical design and digital problem solving skills, my concentration helped educate me about digital problem solving. Not only am I learning about the social and technical aspects of information systems, I am being taught the intricacies that go into developing and implementing the most popular systems for distributing information. With this education I will be able to take knowledge on topics I find interesting and apply it to make a meaningful impact. 

I want to design software and video games for a living. I want to make improvements that will have a big impact on helping people. I want to help design and run the software that helped me become the person I am today and I want to make sure that the future generations are getting a better chance than I did to make connections with people and learn who they want to be. Since so many people use software of all kinds in their everyday life it is extremely important that digital tools be designed thoughtfully and with their real life use in mind. I want to work on something I can be proud of and show off to other people. That’s why I want to focus on making something impactful and connect people across the world.
